# Known Issues And Future Work

## Current Known Issues

- There is no UI yet for lap, position, countdown or results.
- Power-up gameplay is not implemented yet.
- Bots are still using a heuristic controller, not reinforcement learning.
- The prototype scene generator creates a logic-first graybox rather than a final art track.

## Planned Future Work

- Add power-up assignment and item usage.
- Integrate ML-Agents with documented training results.
- Add player stats logging and C# classifier rules.
- Add a results screen and final presentation flow.
- Build at least one visually clearer low-poly circuit.
